2. Scope and privacy principles

This policy applies to the Norwaymaxxing iOS app and this legal website. Norwaymaxxing is a lifestyle and wellness product. It does not use HealthKit, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or advertising identifiers.

We do not sell personal data, serve third-party advertising, or use personal data for cross-context behavioural advertising. We limit collection to operating the app, validating subscriptions, understanding onboarding and paywall performance, maintaining security, and improving the product.

4. Purposes and legal bases

  • Provide the app and remember your progress: performance of the service requested by you and our legitimate interest in delivering a functional device-local experience.
  • Process purchases and restore Premium access: performance of the subscription contract, compliance with legal obligations, and prevention of fraud.
  • Measure onboarding, paywall, and product performance: our legitimate interest in understanding whether the product works, improving it, and maintaining a sustainable service. The analytics are not used for advertising or third-party profiling.
  • Security, support, and legal claims: our legitimate interests in protecting the service, responding to requests, and establishing, exercising, or defending legal claims.
  • Compliance: processing necessary to meet accounting, tax, consumer-protection, and other legal obligations.

If we introduce processing that relies on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting processing already carried out lawfully.

International processing

6. Data retention and deletion

  • Device-local profile and progress data remain until you reset the app data or uninstall the app.
  • Analytics are retained only while reasonably necessary to measure product performance, investigate issues, and compare releases, after which they are deleted or aggregated.
  • Subscription and entitlement records are retained while access is active and afterwards as needed for restore, fraud prevention, disputes, legal claims, and mandatory accounting or tax periods.
  • Support and rights-request correspondence is retained for the time needed to resolve the request and document compliance.

You can reset the local profile from the developer controls in non-production builds or remove local app data by uninstalling Norwaymaxxing. Uninstalling does not automatically cancel an App Store subscription or erase transaction records held by Apple.

7. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location and the applicable law, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to processing. You may also withdraw consent where processing relies on consent.

Requests can be sent to the controller's postal address in section 1. We may ask for proportionate information to verify the request and locate the relevant records. If we cannot identify data as relating to you, we will explain that limitation.

If you are in the EEA, you may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. In Poland, this is the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (UODO).

8. Children

Norwaymaxxing is intended for adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. A person under 18 should not perform cold exposure, sauna, breathwork, or dietary changes through the app without permission and appropriate supervision from a parent or guardian and, where relevant, medical guidance.
